Our Process

Simple, transparent workflow ‚Äî from first call to final delivery.

Discovery & Brief

Short call / meeting jahan hum aapka business, goals aur audience samajhte hain. Is stage par requirements clear karte hain aur scope define hota hai.

Strategy & Design

Moodboards, wireframes ya design concepts taiyar kiye jaate hain. Aap feedback dete hain, hum refine karte hain jab tak direction perfect na ho jaaye.

Build / Develop

Approved direction ke baad final designs banaye jaate hain, assets export hote hain, aur agar web project hai to frontend dev / handoff bhi iss stage par hota hai.

Deliver & Support

Final files, documentation aur guidelines deliver ki jaati hain. Launch ke baad bhi hum minor tweaks, updates aur support ke liye available rehte hain.
